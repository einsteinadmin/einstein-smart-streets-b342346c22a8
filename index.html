SMART STREETS

The Einstein driving-habits arcade. Same rules as the road: create space and visibility, buy yourself time.
👀
Eyes up — read the lights ahead The strip up top shows the next two signals. If you can't see ahead, slow down.
🛟
Surround yourself with space Protect the cushion around your truck. Ease off and roll on to keep traffic out of it — and when you do stop, stop 15 feet back.
🚦
Light pacing — try to never stop "When a light is green, prepare to slow. When a light is red, prepare to go." Time it so you're still rolling. Stop signs are the opposite — those are ALWAYS a full stop.
